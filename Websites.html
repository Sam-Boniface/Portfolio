Joanne Boniface - Portfolio

The first website I built.
This site was designed as an interactive portfolio demonstrating a range of kitchen designs and to give an overview of the client's background.
It has a detailed profile section which is used to promote the client's capabilities in the workplace and provides a number of notable achievements as well as a location to download their CV. It also has a number of images based on a variety of ranges that show different projections of a customers' kitchen designs (almost all of which went on to be purchased and installed).
