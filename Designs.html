Dangerous Dave - Dino Rescue

Premise

In Dino Rescue you take on the role of Dave. Dave is small but dangerous dinosaur who, along with his friends, must escape the clutches of the vile alien Xenon.

After springing a daring escape, Dave quickly discovers he is alone and trapped on an alien planet.
In the distance he can hear his friends calling out for help.
Dave wastes no time setting off in pursuit of the villainous captors, intent on saving his friends and escaping back to Earth.

Dangerous Dave - Space Race

Dave is a dinosaur on a mission to save his friends from the clutches of the evil Xenon, a ruthless alien intent on using Earth as an illegal breeding site.
Will Dave be able to save his friends and escape back to Earth or will it be too late?

Premise

In Space Race you take on the role of Dave. Dave and his friends have stolen space ships and are now racing to escape the alien leader Xenon.
Dave and his friends must traverse otherworldly obstacles and avoid perilous hazards all whilst trying to evade capture.
A karting game in space, you must race across the cosmos battling Xenon and avoiding alien space pirates. Maybe you'll get back to Earth before dinner time.

Galactic Growth

Farming your way to freedom.
You alone can save the galaxy.

Premise

Galactic Growth is a 3rd person farming game where you must travel across the galaxy exploring strange new planets in search of the perfect farming conditions.
Your goal, earn enough money to save your galaxy from the evil Raxel, an alien intent on buying the galaxy to re-purpose it as an intergalactic mall.
The townsfolk of the galaxy are counting on you. Have you got the green fingers required to rebuild the failing farming community.

Isolated

It's out there, somewhere, in the darkness.
I'm alone now... freezing... trapped...

Premise

Isolated is a 3rd person survival horror game set in a remote ski-lodge cut off by an avalanche.
You take on the role of Matthew Rogers, the 'lucky' winner of an all-expense paid skiing trip; now clinging on to survival.
There is no power, no heating, and a blizzard has cut off any chance of escape.
You're trapped and must salvage anything you can to survive the freezing temperatures for the next 5 days. And you're not alone...

Outbreak

Witness recount of day one of the outbreak

It was just a day like any other. I got up, got dressed and went to work. It was Tuesday... at lunch... that's when it happened.
It was like the man was crazed, he started attacking this woman, pinned her to the ground.
People rushed over to help, they dragged him off her, but that only made things worse, the man started attacking others.
Eventually he was restrained but apparently he had bitten several of the people trying to restrain him.
He must have infected them or something... they all fell to the ground fitting.
When it they finally stopped fitting, they all seemed dead, until they got to their feet.
That's when the outbreak really took hold.
